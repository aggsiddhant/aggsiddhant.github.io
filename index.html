About

Polymath in progress.

I’m a happy jack-of-all-trades moving between physics, psychology, machines, software, and medicine. The goal isn’t to collect labels. It’s to build things that make life less stupid and more sustainable.

I like working where disciplines collide: a CNC machine that shapes steel, a solar boiler that cuts energy use by orders of magnitude, a vacuum dryer that makes herbal extraction actually affordable, a website that makes classical Ayurveda accessible, a counseling session that nudges someone onto a better path.

I believe in protecting the earth and the messy biodiversity we haven’t fully understood yet. “Growth” that burns through people and ecosystems doesn’t impress me. Neither do performative “impact” narratives or cosmetic sustainability.

I have a low tolerance for fakeness, buzzword-stacked decks, and the idea that a LinkedIn headline is the same thing as real work. I’d rather show you a working prototype, a line balanced on a real shop floor, or a patient feeling better on a classical formulation.

This site is where I document what I’m building, what I’m learning, what breaks along the way, and how all of this connects back to being a halfway decent human in a very weird century.

Projects

Selected things I’ve built or led.

A mix of hardware, processes, and platforms across fabrication, herbal extraction, energy, and community work.

Hardware & Manufacturing

CNC & Machine Development

Project Lead — Kairos Bansal Pvt. Ltd.

Directed in-house development of plasma cutters, CO₂ CNC setups, a metal 3D printer, and 5-axis milling systems. Focused on designing machines that are affordable enough for small shops while still precise and reliable.

Reduced fabrication costs by ~50% and increased operational efficiency by ~35% through custom frames, motion systems, and control architecture tuned to real use, not catalog specs.

CNC Automation Cost Engineering
Energy & Thermal Systems

Solar Boiler for Process Heat

Design Lead — R&D

Engineered a custom solar concentrator and thermal system aimed at replacing conventional boilers for low-to-medium temperature industrial processes.

Achieved up to ~99% savings in direct energy usage for targeted applications and designed it to be manufacturable and deployable at small industrial scale, not just lab-demonstration level.

Solar Thermal Design Sustainability
Circular Systems

Cellulose Waste → Biogas & Sugar

R&D Lead

Built a pilot process to convert ~100 kg/month of cellulose-rich waste into fermentable sugars and biogas, cutting disposal costs while recovering usable energy and materials.

Established in-house cellulase production, reduced disposal costs by essentially 100% for that stream, and trimmed fuel needs by around 5% while opening new value-add pathways.

Bioprocess Waste-to-Value Energy
Herbal Extraction

Vacuum Dryers & Extraction Systems

MD R&D — Shri Krishna Ayur Siddhi

Designed and built high-efficiency extraction and drying equipment: custom electric kettles, granulators, mortars, stirrers, and vacuum dryers tuned for herbal formulations.

Cut drying costs by ~60%, reduced solvent loss by ~50%, and increased extraction yields by ~12%, translating to ~15% lower processing cost using variations of the Soxhlet method.

Ayurveda Process Design Cost Reduction
Instrumentation

Low-Cost Weighing & Handling Systems

R&D & Implementation

Built custom weighing systems and material-handling solutions, including in-house forklifts tailored to cramped, real-world spaces instead of catalog layouts.

Achieved ~91% savings versus market equipment by building weighing systems at ~9% of standard cost, while maintaining accuracy and reliability for daily operations.
